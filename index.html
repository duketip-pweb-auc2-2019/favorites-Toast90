About

The Simpsons is a well known TV show that features a family, called the Simpsons. In the unlikely event that anyone here does not know what the Simpsons is generally about, it is like an animated sitcom.

Marge Simpsons: Mother of the Simpsons children. Has amazing hair.


Bart Simpson: Oldest of the Simpson children. A real prankster.
